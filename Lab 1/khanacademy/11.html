Pasta Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta 150g
Eggs 4
Parmesan Cheese 50g
Garlic 2 cloves
Salt & Pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.

Step 2: In a large pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.

Step 3: In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese.

Step 4: Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan with the pancetta. Remove from heat.

Step 5: Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the spaghetti, stirring quickly to create a creamy sauce. Season with salt and pepper.

Greek Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cucumber 1
Tomatoes 4
Red Onion 1
Feta Cheese 200g
Olives 100g
Olive Oil 3 tbsp
Oregano 1 tsp
Salt & Pepper To taste

Step 1: Chop the cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion into bite-sized pieces.

Step 2: In a large bowl, combine the chopped vegetables with feta cheese and olives.

Step 3: Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.

Tomato Basil Soup

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 1kg
Onion 1
Garlic 3 cloves
Vegetable Broth 500ml
Basil Leaves 1 cup
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Salt & Pepper To taste

Step 1: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until soft.

Step 2: Add chopped tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.

Step 3: Stir in basil leaves and season with salt and pepper. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.

Step 4: Serve hot, garnished with additional basil leaves if desired.
